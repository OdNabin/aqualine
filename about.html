AQUALINE water technology
AQUALINE Water Technology, an Engineering Office of the Pollet Water Group (PWG) since its inception in 1975 in Belgium, we specialize in providing cutting-edge water and wastewater treatment solutions. Our comprehensive range of solutions spans industrial, commercial, medical, and domestic applications, ensuring a diverse array of water treatment needs are met.
​
Over three decades, Pollet Water Group has been dedicated to thinking about water. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life, improve the comfort of doing business, and increase the efficiency of industrial processes through a network of specialized water treatment companies. Our expertise and solutions ensure the delivery of perfectly treated water for various applications, including buildings, businesses, factories, hospitals, and more. Pollet Water Group provides both individual components and complete solutions across Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.
